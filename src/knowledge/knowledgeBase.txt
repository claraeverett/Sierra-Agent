# Sierra Outdoors Adventure Company - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  

## About Sierra Outdoors  

What is Sierra Outdoors Adventure Company?  
Sierra Outdoors is a premier retailer of outdoor gear and adventure equipment. We specialize in high-quality products for hiking, camping, backpacking, and extreme outdoor activities. Our goal is to provide outdoor enthusiasts with durable, reliable, and innovative gear for all their adventures.  

Where are you located?  
Sierra Outdoors is an online-based retailer, with our main warehouse and fulfillment center located in Denver, Colorado. While we do not have physical retail locations at this time, we ship across the U.S. and Canada.  

What are your business hours?  
Our customer support team is available during the following hours:  
- Monday - Friday: 8 AM - 6 PM PT  
- Saturday: 10 AM - 4 PM PT  
- Sunday: Closed  

Our online store is open 24/7, so you can shop at your convenience.  

## Ordering & Shipping  

What shipping options do you offer?  
We offer the following shipping methods:  
- Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days  
- Expedited Shipping: 1-2 business days  
- Overnight Shipping: Next-day delivery  

Shipping times may vary depending on location and carrier availability.  

Do you offer international shipping?  
At this time, we ship to the U.S. and Canada. We are working on expanding our international shipping options in the near future.  

How can I track my order?  
Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to follow your package's journey. If you need assistance tracking your order, you can contact our customer service team.  

Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?  
Orders can only be modified or canceled if they have not yet been processed for shipping. Please contact customer support as soon as possible if you need to make changes to your order.  

## Returns & Exchanges  

What is your return policy?  
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase as long as the item is unused, in its original packaging, and in resellable condition. Customized items are final sale and cannot be returned.  

How do I initiate a return?  
To start a return, visit our Returns & Exchanges Portal on our website or contact our customer service team for assistance. You will need your order number and email to process the request.  

Do you offer exchanges?  
Yes, we offer exchanges for items of equal or lesser value. If you need a different size, color, or model, you can request an exchange when initiating your return.  

What if my item arrives damaged or defective?  
Contact us within 48 hours of receiving your item, and we will send a replacement at no additional cost.
